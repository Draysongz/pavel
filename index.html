What is $DUROV?

$DUROV is a community-driven memecoin on @ton_blockchain, inspired by Pavel Durov - the founder of Telegram. We took Durov's name out of respect for him, having created the most successful social networking site of all time. We created it as a thank you to him.

3. Go to Ston.fi
connect to Ston.fi. Go to Ston.fi in google chrome. Connect your wallet. Paste the $DUROV token address EQB22u_EPF5DyqtZRhjJ2YiZliN-w5ZCq6F_PY6Ia6bvi3Et into Ston.fi, select $DUROV, and confirm. When Tonkeeper prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

4. Switch TON for $DUROV
